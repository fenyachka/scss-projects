Tbilisi WAMS school on Algebra and Cryptography for students

The University of Georgia, Tbilisi, Georgia June 22 - June 30, 2020

The Tbilisi school is intended to propose a variety of introductory topics in Algebra and Cryptography to attract local graduate students and participants from neighbouring countries.

The application procedure for International participants it will be activated on January 15th, 2020. Georgian applicants should contact the local organizers.
